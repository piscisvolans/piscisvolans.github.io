Ask the Van is the public Q&A service of the Department of Physics at the University of Illinois. Anyone can submit a question, and physics professors and student volunteers answer as many as possible. (It's called Ask the Van because of the Physics Van, a traveling science demo program also run by the physics department.)

I keep track of the questions I've answered, but check out the main site for lots of other great topics that I don't know enough about...
